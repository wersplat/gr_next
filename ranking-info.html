Ranking System Information

System Overview

The NBA 2K Pro Am Global Rankings tracks and compares competitive team performance across Franchise League, UPA College, UPA-sanctioned events, and verified external circuits. Our system provides a unified RP (Ranking Points) structure to evaluate teams fairly across different regions and formats.

RP Categories

Event RP

Earned through placements in LANs, tournaments, and qualifiers.

Max: 1000 RP per event

Franchise RP

Accumulated through weekly matches and season achievements.

Includes wins, top 10 victories, and clean sweeps

Bonus RP

Awarded for special achievements and performances.

Includes MVPs, undefeated runs, and clutch plays

Verified League RP

Points from UPA College and other verified leagues.

Subject to seasonal caps
